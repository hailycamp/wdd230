Fitt's Law

Superlist

Website Link

This page exemplifies the streamlined user experience that Fitt's Law can offer. The elements on the page, particularly the buttons and text field, are large and easily accessible. Fitt's Law suggests this causes users to select them quicker and easier. In addition, there is a very small about of space between the text field and submission button, meaning less travel time and energy between selections.
