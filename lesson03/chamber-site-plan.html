Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Details

Site Name

Kailua Town Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Kailua Chamber of Commerce site is a hub for the city's economic domain. The site seeks to promote local business. It will encourage the exchange of both commerce and ideas to support the development and beautification of Kailua for the benefit of residents and tourists alike.
