Demographics

Kailua is home to 40,400 people, 97.6% of which are citizens. As of 2021, 8.75% of Kailua residents were born outside of the country. In 2021, there were 1.71 times more White (Non-Hispanic) residents (16.7k people) in Kailua, HI than any other race or ethnicity.
