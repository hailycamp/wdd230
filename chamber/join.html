Membership Benefits

NP Membership

$0/year

This level is for non-profit organizations and has no fee. It includes the same benefits as a Silver Member.

Bronze Membership

$155/year

You get access to quarterly mixers, professional development workshops, and 10% off business insurance premiums contracted through Kailua's own, Jack Wolfe Insurance Inc.

Silver Membership

$225/year

You get access to everything from the Bronze membership, plus 30% off ad space in the Honolulu Star-Advertiser, 25% off insurance premiums through Jack Wolfe Insurance Inc., and direct communication with our chamber's own board of legal advisors.

Gold Membership

$415/year

You get access to everything in the Silver membership, plus quarterly marketing at local grocery stores, vip reserved tents at all Kailua & Lanikai farmer's markets, and spotlight space on our town chamber's very own website.
