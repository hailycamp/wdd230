Honda Metropolitan

This 49cc scooter can seat 1 passenger. It features a step-through design, no-shift atuomatic transmission, and an electric starter for easy operation. There is also lockable under-seat storage. This incredibly fuel-efficient scooter makes travel simple, easy, and fun.

Honda Dio

This scooter seats 2 passengers and has a 110cc tank. It features a sleek design and intelligent digital meter so you can visualize real-time technical updates about your scooter, such as mileage, distance to empty, and battery. This is the fastest and most sporty scooter we offer, at 149cc, getting you up to 70 mph. The PCX150 may seat up to 2 passengers, includes underseat storage, and tons of legroom.

This fully-decked out version of the Jeep wrangler comes with all the exploratory benefits of the 2 door, but with further options for comfort, like A/C for the truly hot and humid days.
